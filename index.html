About Me

I have always had a passion for IT and after completing my GCSE A-Level, I joined with a college in Sri Lanka to do the Level 5 BTEC HND in Software Development. After successful completion of HND, I enrolled with the University of Greenwich to top up my HND with BSc Hons Business IT and graduated in 2011. However, after graduation, I worked outside of the IT industry for about 6 years.

I always wanted to get back into IT and in 2017 I decided to update myself with the latest technologies and enrolled with the University of Hertfordshire to study BSc Hons in Computer Science. After graduating in 2018, I needed to advance my technical skills to turn my passion into reality. I joined with an IT training provider called Just IT to learn new technical skills and to advance my existing skills. This is a fully self-funded programme where I learned HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, C# and ASP.Net.
